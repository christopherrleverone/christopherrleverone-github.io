    All About Us

    Simply put, we're the home for public broadcasting in Franklin, MA. We are a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit business funded by... cable subscriptions! That's right: Comcast and Verizon invest in the communities they serve, and it's your cable-given right to make content for TV and radio!

    Our home base is at 23 Hutchinson St. Here we operate three cable TV stations, one radio station, and free online livestreams. You can also find us out in the wild, covering local events. We have an extensive catalogue of shows from every genre, and it's expanding every day; all because of our friendly volunteers!

    Our staff is deeply knowledgeable about in-studio production and on-site coverage. Get to know them a little!
