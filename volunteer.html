    Volunteer!

    At Franklin TV & WFPR, volunteers are at the center of everything we do. Without them, there would be no content!

    Luckily, we've built a network of friends and neighbors who want to help make 'community media' the best it can be. We take pride in their creativity, technical acheivements and insight into the production process.

    Want to learn how to host a program or use basic A/V tech? Send us an email or stop by for coffee! Workshops are available through signups and we're always answering questions.

    Happy producing! ☺
